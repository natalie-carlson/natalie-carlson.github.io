About Me

I'm currently a doctoral student in Management at Columbia Business School in New York. My research interests include social entrepreneurship, technology, and innovation, particularly in developing and emerging economies. My work draws on a variety of methodologies, with a focus on new computational methods and text as data. Prior to starting at Columbia, I worked as a research associate for Innovations for Poverty Action (IPA), managing field experiments in Liberia, Uganda, and Ethiopia. I received my bachelor's degree in Economics from Yale University.
